Terms & Conditions

These Terms & Conditions govern access to the RentGear website, reservation-related communications, account creation, and any resulting rental transaction. By using the website or submitting information through it, the customer agrees to these Terms, the Privacy Policy, and any booking-specific conditions later issued by RentGear.

  1. Vehicle listings are informational and do not guarantee final availability until confirmed by RentGear.
  2. Customers must provide accurate, current, and complete information in connection with accounts, inquiries, and reservations.
  3. RentGear may decline or cancel a booking where availability changes, verification fails, documents are incomplete, payment is not received, or the requested use presents unacceptable operational or legal risk.
  4. Displayed pricing may exclude deposits, tolls, fuel replacement, extensions, delivery fees, late return charges, damage-related amounts, and other lawful adjustments described in the applicable policy set or rental paperwork.
  5. Customers are responsible for reviewing their booking details carefully before final confirmation.

Privacy Policy

RentGear may collect personal information such as customer name, email address, contact number, driver's license details, account data, reservation information, and customer communications for operational, legal, and service-related purposes.

RentGear intends to process personal information in accordance with applicable privacy law, including the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 and relevant rules and issuances. Data handling should reflect transparency, legitimate purpose, proportionality, and appropriate security safeguards.

Purpose of processing

  • To create and manage customer accounts
  • To review and process booking requests
  • To verify identity and supporting details
  • To communicate about reservations, incidents, support, and payments
  • To maintain legal, accounting, and dispute-resolution records

Disclosure and service providers

Data may be shared with payment processors, hosting providers, cloud services, insurance partners, repair partners, advisers, or other providers where reasonably necessary for business operations and subject to appropriate controls.

Retention and security

Personal data should be kept only for as long as reasonably necessary for the stated purposes, contractual needs, legal obligations, and legitimate claims-related handling. Reasonable technical, organizational, and physical safeguards should be used to protect stored and transmitted data.

Insurance Policy

Insurance or vehicle protection may apply to certain units, but coverage scope, exclusions, limits, deductibles, and claims procedures depend on the actual policy and booking conditions. Customers should not assume that all loss, theft, damage, delay, cargo issues, or third-party claims are fully covered.

  • Prompt reporting and accurate documentation may be required.
  • Coverage may be reduced or denied for prohibited, reckless, illegal, or unauthorized use.
  • The renter may remain responsible for deductibles, excluded losses, downtime, or uninsured damage.

Rental Agreement

Each confirmed booking should ultimately be supported by a rental agreement or release form that identifies the vehicle, rental period, price, deposit, authorized driver details, and any specific restrictions or commercial-use conditions.

  1. The renter must inspect the vehicle at handoff and raise visible discrepancies promptly.
  2. The renter must use the vehicle lawfully, carefully, and only for approved purposes.
  3. Unauthorized drivers, sub-renting, illegal transport, racing, overloading, and misuse are prohibited unless expressly approved in writing.
  4. Extensions require prior approval from RentGear.

Damage Policy

The renter must return the vehicle in substantially the same condition as released, ordinary wear and tear excepted. Accidents, theft, vandalism, flood exposure, and any material damage should be reported immediately.

Incident expectations

  • Stop use if continued operation is unsafe.
  • Notify RentGear promptly.
  • Preserve photos, receipts, reports, and relevant third-party details.
  • Follow any reporting or claims steps required by the booking terms or insurance arrangement.

Chargeable amounts may include repair costs, deductibles, towing, loss-of-use amounts where lawfully supported, cleaning, restoration, and administrative handling costs where applicable.
