About Us

RentGear is designed as a practical car and truck rental service for customers in Paranaque and nearby Metro Manila business districts. The goal is to keep the booking experience clear, the fleet dependable, and the supporting policies visible before a customer commits.

Our operating approach centers on transparent rates, documented vehicle condition, and straightforward customer communication. That matters for both personal-use rentals and commercial vehicle bookings where timing, paperwork, and accountability are important.

Our Fleet

RentGear offers passenger vehicles for everyday travel and truck units for delivery, hauling, events, and project support. Fleet availability may vary depending on maintenance cycles, prior bookings, route suitability, and release requirements.
